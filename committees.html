Committees

Our committees have their own meeting times. If you wish to attend a meeting, please send us an email and we'll get you in touch with the committee directors!

International Projects

The International Projects Committee focuses on raising money to support international charities. In the past we have done burger and beer fundraisers for the APU Malawi Girls on the Move School, and raised money for various natural disaster relief efforts.

Local Projects

The Local Projects Committee focuses on positive service within our community. Generally that means volunteering and fundraising within Greater Victoria, be it beach cleanups, cigarette butt pickups, donations of warm clothing and hygiene items to shelters, or sponsoring hot lunches at Our Place. We also raise money for the BC Children’s Hospital Festival of Trees, showing that to us community means embracing the entire province.

Literacy

The Literacy Committee is in charge of promoting literacy within Greater Victoria. This means sending members to the Literacy Round Tables, working closely with the Thousand by Five charity, and participating in Story Studio.

Public Relations

The PR Committee is in charge of projecting and promoting the club’s image. This is done through social media posts about our various events, spotlights on club members, lip dub videos to promote our club, and the occasional partnership with other committees on their events (ie holding a Rotaract PR/Info booth at a fundraiser or Rotary convention).

Professional Development

The Professional Development Committee is in charge of arranging speakers and workshops for the Rotaract Club. Speakers from a wide range of organizations have visited the club, and we have put on numerous speed networking events. Some of the speakers include members from local Rotary Clubs, Mercy Ships International, and Disaster Aid.

Club Services

The Club Services Committee is in charge of setting up and taking down the meetings, and organizing socials for the club members. This involves setting up banners, setting out tea, arranging snacks, and clearing up after the meeting. Some of the fun socials that have been held in the past include laser tag, potlucks, club nights, board game nights, skating, and afternoon coffee.
